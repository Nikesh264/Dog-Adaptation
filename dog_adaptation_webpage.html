Our Adoption Process

Step 1: Browse Available Dogs

Visit our shelter or browse our online gallery to find dogs that match your lifestyle and preferences. Each dog profile includes information about their breed, age, temperament, and special needs.

Step 2: Submit an Application

Once you've found a potential match, complete our adoption application form. This helps us ensure that each dog goes to a home that can meet their specific needs.

Step 3: Meet and Greet

Schedule a time to meet your potential new family member! This is an opportunity for you to interact with the dog and see if you're a good match for each other.

Step 4: Home Check

For some dogs, we conduct a brief home check to ensure your living situation is appropriate for the specific needs of the dog you're interested in adopting.

Step 5: Adoption Fee and Paperwork

Pay the adoption fee which covers vaccinations, microchipping, and spay/neuter procedures. Complete the necessary paperwork to make the adoption official.

Step 6: Welcome Home!

Take your new best friend home! We'll provide resources and support to help your new dog adjust to their forever home.
